Welcome to GMIT indoor navigation!

This app will help you navigate inside GMIT Dublin campus bulding complex.

Map section.Helps you get your bearings on surounding GMIT grounds. It will be centered on on bulidng and in future verion will include building layout. Your current locaton is marked red

Navigate section. Indoor navigations have difficulty with accurate location aquiring through GPS system. This app is designed will give you direction from the room you are currently in, or near by, to your destination room. Room numbers can be located on top of every door. Navigation is designed to give direction from the point you leaving the room, that means you are facing the corridor and have your back to the room.
